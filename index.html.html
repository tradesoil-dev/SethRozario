About

Every frame is a conversation with light.

Yahweh Production is a photography studio rooted in faith, creativity, and a deep love for storytelling. Based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, we bring a distinct eye to every shoot — whether it's an intimate portrait, a vibrant event, or the quiet beauty of the world around us.

With professional-grade gear and a heart for meaningful imagery, every photograph is crafted to tell a story you'll want to keep forever.

Seth Rozario
Photographer · Yahweh Production
